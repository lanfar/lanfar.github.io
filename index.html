    About Us

    Lanfar Nig. LTD provides specialized Building/ construction services in different areas of Engineering with particular emphasis on Building, Civil, highway, Roads, Bridges, water and waste disposal Construction Engineering. The services offered covered feasibility studies, technical surveys, and investigations, Construction, Rehabilitation and Renovations. Lanfar Nig. LTD have varied experience in many fields of engineering and offer services, which combine a broad technical background with an objective view, directed towards protecting the interest of our clients and ensuring superior engineering/ Construction work at reasonable cost. The company stirs diligently to meet the needs of clients in both the public and private sectors using the latest and most advanced techniques in construction engineering.

    Our Mission

    Recognizing the growing requirement for inter-disciplinary approach to technological problems and thus employs a large number of professionals as full time/ part time construction and consulting specialist with relevant practical experience in all fields of Engineering and construction technology and management.

    Our Plan

    In order to meet the broad needs of technological and industrial development, it has become necessary that groups of professionals with diverse background work together in an integrated team.

    Our Vision

    We utilize inputs from this vast reservoir of talents as and when required. The search for additional inputs is not restricted to Nigeria alone, but extended to foreign counterpart or and Specialized expatriate company or partners where absolutely necessary.

    Services

    As construction specialist of repute, we offer our services to government agencies, private sector and various individual clients in the collective task of transforming the economy of the nation by using various tools of science and technology. We strongly believe that "There is no problem without a solution". LANFAR NIG. LTD offers services in the following field of construction:

    Construction, Renovation/ Rehabilitation of Roads, bridges and other Highway structures.

    Construction of Residential, office and Industrial buildings.

    Construction/ Rehabilitation of water supplies and sewage disposal facilities.

    Irrigation Works facilities & Borehole drilling.

    Construction of Dams and related hydraulic structures.

    Flood and Erosion control.

    Reconnaissance survey, identification / formulation of project.
